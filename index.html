Manta Ray

Mantas became strictly protected in international waters because of their inclusion in the Convention on Migratory Species of Wild Animals.

  • Scientific Name: Mobulidae
  • Maximum Width: 7 meter
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Habitat: warm temperated waters

They are filter feeders and eat large quantities of zooplankton, which they swallow with their open mouths as they swim.
